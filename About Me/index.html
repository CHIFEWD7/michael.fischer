What is a Hyper Fisch?

My "real name" is Michael Fischer. As a person I am most easily explained by the bulleted factoids above. Hyper Fisch is this way-cool pseudonym I gave myself in an attempt at explaing those bullets within a singular word. Any real human would probably wonder why some basic graphic designer needs a fake name. The excuse I give myself is that when I am not in the office slinging the sexiest of graphics or in the studio directing top-notch photo shoots, I can be found running around the streets of Chicago snapping photos in places I probably shouldn't be. It is my way of creating street art in way that's original to me, with my camera. AKA making momma proud everyday.

Currently my big project is what I am calling "The Chicago Project." For more details on this project or work enquiries feel free to reach out to me via email at hyperfish@gmail.com.
